Coconut Buns Filling

Yummy coconut buns have coconut filling inside Delightful and satisfying bringing this to a party will have everybody impressed.

Ingredients

  • The Bread Dough
  • 2/3C Heavy Cream
  • 1 C milk
  • 1 Large egg
  • 1/3 C sugar
  • 1/2 C cake flour
  • 3.5C bread flour
  • 1 tb active dry yeast
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • For the Filling
  • 6 tb of butter
  • 3 tb of cake flour
  • 1/4 C Dry milk powder
  • 1/2 C desiccated coconut
  • Toppings
  • 1/3C cake flour
  • 3 tb butter
  • 4.5 tp caster sugar
  • egg wash
  • 1 tb sesame seeds
  • 1 tb sugar
  1. Directions
  2. Mix heavy cream, milk, eff, sugar, cake flour, bread flour, yeast, and salt (in this order
  3. Leave out for 15 min
  4. Combine all filling ingredients
  5. After bread out for 1 hr, put dough into mixer and knead for 5 min
  6. Flatten dough and roll into flat oval, add filling and roll into a cigar
  7. allow 45 minutes for buns to rise, preheat oven to 350 mix topping ingredients to zip loc bag
  8. Once done in oven bursh with topping.
