BerryCream

A refreshing bowl of juicy berries and cream, where the berries sit atop of a light but decadent orange infused whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup strawberries
  • I cup rasberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 C sugar
  • 1.2 C fresh orange juice
  • 2 C Heavy c=whipping cream
  1. Combine the sugar, orange juice and rind if possile into a small pan
  2. Heat and bring to a boil until sugar dissolves
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes witout stirring
  4. Cool syrup and whip cream to medium peaks
  5. Fold orange syrup into whip cream
  6. Serve over berries
  7. If you making normal cream, add 1 tsp of vanilla and 2-3 tbsp powdered sugar and whip until soft peaks form
