Power salad

Indulge in a satisfying and bright salad for breakfast lunch or dinner. The dressing is homemade, and the vegetables absorb every bit of it

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tb lemon juice
  • 1 clove farlic
  • 1/2tp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tp sugar
  • 1/4 tp pepper
  • 4 c torn green leaf lettuce
  • 4 c baby spinach
  • 2 c shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 c halved grape tomatoes
  • 1 c halved and sliced cucumber
  • 1/2 c slivered red onion
  • 1/2 c sliced pepperoncini
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2tb toasted unsalted sunflower seeds

Directions

  1. whisk oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  2. Add lettuce, spinach, chicken, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and pepperoncini
